Minimum entry requirement and admission criteria

Thanks for your interest to study with us.

ESGT application and admission processing system is end-to-end online. Prospective students are required to apply and make all relevant payments online. Only qualified individuals who meet the minimum entry requirements are admitted into any program of ESGT University.

Entry requirements for International Students

  • Five relevant SSCE credits including English and Mathematics (Undergraduates)
  • Degree certificate, transcript, and recommendation letter (for Postgraduate, Conversion, and Transfer Students)
  • At least one academic referee
  • Letter of sponsorship
  • Release letter (transfer students)
  • Valid means of identification/E-Passport
  • Police report
  • Certificate of Fitness
  • SSCE certificate verification token / scratch card (undergraduates)
