Postgraduate Studies

Graduate study and research opportunity at ESGT university.

ESGT provides graduates the opportunity to further their studies at a university with high research excellence and reputation while enjoying the rich culture, opportunities, and serenity of Africa’s small but economically giant nation. We offer unique graduate programs tailored to suit the need and schedules of our students.

Our graduate programs are run either on a full-time (for research-based Ph.D. and Masters Degrees) or flexible part-time basis. These programs are designed to extend the depth of your knowledge and update your professional skills through coursework and research.
