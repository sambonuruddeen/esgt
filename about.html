ESGT At A Glance

École Supérieur de Gestion et de Technologie (ESGT-Benin University) is a private university located in Cotonou, Benin Republic. We are proudly one of the trailblazing universities in West Africa offering globally recognized programs in Arts, Sciences, and Management.

Founded in 2009, ESGT is geared towards accelerating economic growth and prosperity in Africa through the advancement of learning specially to disadvantaged groups and communities. ESGT is accredited and licensed by the Ministry of Higher Education and Research of Benin Republic in 2013, and ever since we have grown steadfastly as the preferred destination of students from different parts of Nigeria, Togo, and Niger Republic.

Our Story

Since its inception in 2009, ESGT became the favorite destination of students from most West African countries. Young vibrant learners have come here to challenge the barriers to tertiary education in their home countries. At ESGT, we have an open-door policy because we care about the future of Africa and its people..

We offer academic programs strategically designed to help students excel and remain relevant in this fast-changing world. Our programs range from Social Sciences, Management Sciences and Science and Technology.
